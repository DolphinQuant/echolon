# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## In short

Be kind. Disagree on technical substance, not on people. Trading-system bugs are subtle and reviewers are doing you a favour when they push back hard — assume good faith on both sides.

## Reporting

Conduct issues: email <zongjiany@outlook.com>. Reports stay confidential. We will respond within 7 days.

The full Contributor Covenant text applies. See the link above for the formal document.
